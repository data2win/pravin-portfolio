01 — About

Where data meets ground reality

Over the last 5+ years, I have worked closely with government institutions in Madhya Pradesh, Delhi, Pune, and Daman (DNH&DD) to improve educational outcomes through actionable, data-backed insights.

Currently at ConveGenius.AI, I serve as Project Manager for the Vidya Samiksha Kendra (VSK) in the Union Territory of DNH&DD — shaping data-driven policymaking and last-mile EdTech delivery for 140,000+ students across all government schools.

I graduated from the Young India Fellowship (2024–25) at Ashoka University, where I pursued multidisciplinary research and contributed to a critical pedagogy curriculum. I am open to consulting, research collaborations, and fellowship opportunities.

I believe the future of education reform lies at the intersection of real-time data, AI tools, and grassroots policy understanding — which is exactly where I operate.

05 — Research

NLP + Public Health Discourse

NGIA Final Project Paper · Co-authored with Shweata Hegde
Analyzing Vaccine Discourse in YouTube Comment Sections — A Study of COVID-19 Vaccination in India
Ashoka University Young India Fellowship · 2024–25 · GitHub Repository ↗
Analyzed 4,856 YouTube comments using the YouTube API and OpenAI's GPT-4 for aspect-based sentiment analysis and emotional tagging. Identified anger as the most dominant emotion; anti-vaccine narratives were strongly associated with fear and misinformation. Applied a mixed-method approach combining manual observation, API extraction, and LLM processing to classify sentiments, emotions, and vaccination stances at scale.
